Binocular PD Test

First, hold at the closest distance where the image stays sharp (~10cm) and hold still to calibrate distance. Then point at the RIGHT eye: it beeps and captures automatically when aligned. After each capture, bring the phone to a comfortable distance to check the marker — correct it with a tap if needed — then tap Continue. Slide to the LEFT eye and repeat.

Needs camera permission. Best in good, even lighting. Tap ⟲ to cycle cameras and find your phone's closest-focusing lens (often labelled "macro"). Alignment while up close is audio-only — no need to see the screen until the review step.
