Binocular PD Test

Hold the phone as close as possible to the patient — as close as the camera can still focus. Point the camera at the RIGHT eye first and hold still: it beeps and captures automatically when aligned. Then slide sideways to the LEFT eye and hold still again.

Needs camera permission. Best in good, even lighting. Tap the ⟲ button to cycle cameras — try it to find your phone's closest-focusing lens (often labelled "macro"). Keep the same working distance for both eyes.
