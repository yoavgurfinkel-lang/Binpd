Binocular PD Test

Hold the phone about 30cm from the patient and center both eyes in the two targets. Once aligned it fires a quick burst of frames automatically and picks the best one — then you confirm or nudge the two pupil markers with a tap before it shows Total PD.

Needs camera permission. Best in good, even lighting. Tap ⟲ to cycle cameras.
