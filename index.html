Binocular PD Test

Hold the phone about 30cm from the patient and center both eyes in the two small circular targets. It automatically grabs a fast measurement every moment both eyes line up — no need to hold perfectly still — then averages everything it collected into one Total PD.

Needs camera permission. Best in good, even lighting. Tap ⟲ to cycle cameras.
