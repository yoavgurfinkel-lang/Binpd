Binocular PD Test

Point at the RIGHT eye and hold still — it beeps and captures automatically. Then bring the phone back to a comfortable distance: check the pupil marker and set the midline (nose bridge / glasses center), correcting either with a tap, then tap Continue. Repeat for the LEFT eye.

Needs camera permission. Best in good, even lighting. Tap ⟲ to cycle cameras. Alignment while up close is audio-only — no need to see the screen until the review step. Each eye is measured independently from its own photo, so nothing has to stay lined up between the two.
